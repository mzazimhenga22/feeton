# **App Name**: AuraFlow

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Hero Display: Showcase a futuristic 3D sneaker model floating with cinematic rim lighting, featuring interactive camera controls and detailed textures.
- AI-Powered Micro-Copy Generator: Utilize a generative AI tool to craft elegant, concise taglines and descriptive micro-copy for product highlights, enhancing the luxury brand voice.
- Minimalist Navigation Bar: Implement a clean, unobtrusive header with essential navigation links using ghost button styling.
- Curated Collections Section: Display three sleek product cards with high-definition imagery, soft shadows, and essential product details for luxury sneaker collections.
- Product Quick View/Detail: Enable users to click on product cards for a quick modal view or transition to a detail page with more information.
- Call to Action (CTA): Integrate prominent call-to-action elements for shopping, product inquiry, or brand exploration, styled with neon glowing highlights.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: Vibrant Neon Orange (#FF9900). This high-saturation, high-brightness orange evokes the 'glowing highlights' and dynamic energy of the luxury brand against the dark background.
- Background color: Deep Charcoal Grey (#1F1F1F). This very dark, near-black neutral provides the desired 'deep charcoal and slate grey' aesthetic, offering a sophisticated backdrop for the neon accents.
- Secondary accent color: Fiery Red (#CC1717). This rich, vibrant red is analogous to the primary orange, providing an alternative but complementary 'neon' glow for subtle secondary highlights and interactive elements, ensuring distinction in brightness and saturation from the primary.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, techy, and precise feel, suitable for the 'ultra-modern' and 'sophisticated' look. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clean, neutral, and highly readable qualities, ensuring legibility against a dark background.
- Utilize minimalist, line-based icons for navigation and interactive elements, maintaining the clean and modern UI aesthetic suggested by 'ghost buttons' and 'minimalist navigation bar'.
- Adopt a full-bleed, visually-driven layout with ample negative space to emphasize luxury and sophistication. Strong hierarchy for content sections, featuring large hero imagery and sleek product displays.
- Incorporate subtle glowing and pulsating hover effects on interactive elements like ghost buttons and CTAs. Implement smooth, cinematic transitions for image loading and section reveals, aligning with the 'futuristic' and 'cinematic' visual theme.
